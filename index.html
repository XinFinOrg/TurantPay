Overview

Turant pay is banking solution for instant payments over internet powdered XDC blockchain. XinFin’s Blockchain platform offers integrated payment solutions to enable real time payment and settlement across the globe. The Turant Pay Services can be utilized by individuals, corporates, and financial institutions to facilitate cross border real time payments for trade, financing and remittances. Built on the highly evolved XDC protocol, this solution offers secure and high transaction throughout making it an ideal solution for real world transactions.

Transaction Lifecycle



  • 1. Originating Bank will initiate money transfer Request to Xinfin by using secured messaging layer .

  • 2. Xinfin will create escrow and send acknowledgment to Originating bank for XDC transfer.

  • 3. Xinfin will transfer XDC to Originating bank’s wallet. Also xinfin will deposit same amount of XDC to escrow and send acknowledgment to Originating bank about the same .

  • 4. After getting acknowledgment from Xinfin ,Originating bank will now transfer money to Xinfin’s bank a/c.

  • 5. Originating Bank’s wallet now transfer XDC to beneficiary bank.

  • 6. Once the beneficiary bank will get XDC to its wallet , beneficiary bank will send Acknowledgment Receipt to Bank A via secure messaging layer also handle the payment in fiat currency to customer.

  • 7. Now after handing customer, beneficiary bank will send XDC from its wallet to Xinfin’s wallet. And Xinfin will handle Fiat currency with beneficiary bank.

  • 8. Originating Bank will now send the Acknowledgment Receipt that he got from beneficiary bank to Xinfin for Escrow lock.

  • 9. After getting Acknowledgment Receipt from Originating Bank Xinfin will get Deposited XDC back to its wallet.
