Transaction Lifecycle



  • 1.Originating bank will request financial institute to provide 100$ worth xdc to orignator bank .

  • 2.financial institute will provide XDC worth of 100$ to Originating bank.

  • 3.Originating bank will send XDC worth of 100$ to escrow with beneficiary bank details .

  • 4. After depositing XDC to escrow, acknowledgment will be send to beneficiary bank.

  • 5. beneficiary Bank will transfer amount to end user on beneficiary bank's end.

  • 6. Once the beneficiary bank will send amount to end user , beneficiary bank will send Acknowledgment Receipt to escrow.

  • 7. Upon receiving Acknowledgment from beneficiary bank ,escrow will transfer XDC to beneficiary bank

  • 8. beneficiary bank will now transfer XDC to local financial institute to exchange XDC to native currency.

  • 9. financial institute will send fiat to beneficiary bank.
